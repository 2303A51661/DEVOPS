Why EfficientNetB7?
⚡
Compound Scaling
Balances depth, width and resolution simultaneously — unlike ResNet (depth only) or DenseNet (feature reuse only)
🔍
Higher Resolution
Processes detailed images — essential for detecting small lung nodules in CT scans
∿
Swish Activation
Smoother gradient flow compared to ReLU — leads to better convergence on medical imaging tasks
🎯
Transfer Learning
ImageNet pre-training with first 33 layers frozen — retains general visual features while adapting to CT scans
